MISSION :

London, September 1940;

The Third Reich’s mighty Luftwaffe continues its devastating rain of destruction across Britain, and reports of new and increasingly horrific biological weapons and scientific research undertaken by insane yet brilliant enemy scientists grip the nation. As yet another air raid sweeps across the capital, Londoners take shelter under Waterloo station in the maze of tunnels and vaults hidden deep below the surface of the city, little suspecting that today their lives will change forever.

Deep beneath the city, US and British soldiers join forces to lead their civilian charges through a nightmarish discovery. Dark secrets abound as the survivors must make their way through the cavernous underbelly of a city under siege, down long forgotten tunnels and passages, where every turn could bring them face to face with unknown horrors.

Who will you trust to escort you to safety? …And what frights could be lurking in the deepening dark beneath war-torn London?

Soldiers and scientists will re-tell the previously unheard story of War-time London in this fully immersive, WWII-themed horror story.

Prepare yourself for the Undead Blitz!

You must be physically fit, emotionally sound and ready to step into the unknown!

WARNING: This event is not for the faint-hearted. Expect big frights, gore and the possible invasion of Britain.
